## To create an AWS Academy account

1. Click "Get Started" in the link emailed to your UVA email.
2. Click "Student Login" on the first webpage that appears.
3. Click "Forgot Password?".
4. Enter your UVA email (i.e. ComputingID@virginia.edu) and click "Reset Password".
5. Check your UVA email inbox for an email from AWS Academy.
6. Create a password by clicking on "Click here to set a new password".
7. Then create a password by entering your new password in the text field under "New Password *" and enter the same new password in the text field below "Confirm New Password *".
8. Use this new password and your UVA email address to login to your AWS Academy account.
   
Do note that this is a different Canvas account from the one you access DS 2002 and your other UVA courses from.
If you have any questions please ask in class or come to office hours.
